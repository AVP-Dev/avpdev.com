**Publication Date:** February 4, 2026

This text constitutes a public proposal (offer) by **Patskevich Aliaksei Uladzimirovich**, an individual — payer of tax on professional income (UNP MA6914161), hereinafter referred to as the "Contractor", addressed to an indefinite circle of persons (individuals and legal entities), hereinafter referred to as the "Customer", to conclude an agreement for the provision of services (hereinafter referred to as the "Agreement") on the conditions set forth below.

The publication (posting) of the text of this Agreement on the official website of the Contractor at [https://avpdev.com](https://avpdev.com) constitutes a public proposal (offer) in accordance with Art. 407 of the Civil Code of the Republic of Belarus.

## 1. SUBJECT OF THE AGREEMENT

1.1. The Contractor undertakes to provide the Customer with services in the field of information technology (web development, programming, technical support, interface design), and the Customer undertakes to accept and pay for these services.

1.2. The specific list, scope, deadlines, and cost of Services are agreed upon by the Parties individually via email, messengers (Telegram, Viber, etc.), or during the application process on the website.

## 2. PROCEDURE FOR CONCLUDING THE AGREEMENT

2.1. This Agreement is a public agreement (Art. 396 of the Civil Code of the Republic of Belarus), according to which the Contractor assumes the obligation to provide Services to anyone who applies to them on the agreed terms.

2.2. Unconditional acceptance (acceptance) of the terms of this offer is considered to be the performance by the Customer of one of the following actions:
* Making an advance payment or full payment for the Services based on the Invoice issued by the Contractor;
* Sending the technical assignment or source data to the Contractor to start work after agreeing on the cost;
* Actual consumption of the Services.

## 3. RIGHTS AND OBLIGATIONS OF THE PARTIES

**3.1. The Contractor undertakes:**
* To provide Services qualitatively and within the time frame agreed with the Customer;
* To ensure the confidentiality of data received from the Customer.

**3.2. The Customer undertakes:**
* To provide the Contractor with full and reliable information necessary for the provision of Services (accesses, content, layouts);
* To pay for the Services in the manner and within the terms agreed by the Parties;
* To accept the results of the Services provided.

## 4. SETTLEMENT PROCEDURE AND ACCEPTANCE OF SERVICES

4.1. The cost of Services is not subject to VAT (due to the Contractor's application of a special tax regime — tax on professional income).

4.2. Payment is made by non-cash transfer of funds to the Contractor's account based on the issued Invoice.

4.3. In accordance with the Resolution of the Ministry of Finance of the Republic of Belarus No. 13 dated February 12, 2018, the Contractor has the right to unilaterally draw up a Primary Accounting Document (PAD) confirming the commission of a business transaction. Signing of bilateral acts of acceptance of services is not required unless otherwise agreed by the Parties.

4.4. Services are considered rendered properly and accepted by the Customer if, within 3 (three) calendar days after notification by the Contractor of the completion of work (stage of work), the Customer has not presented written motivated claims.

## 5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

5.1. Exclusive rights to the results of intellectual activity (program code, design) created within the framework of the execution of the Agreement pass to the Customer at the moment of full payment of the cost of Services, unless otherwise agreed by the Parties.

5.2. The Contractor retains the right to use code fragments, libraries, and developments (background technology) created earlier or being universal for other projects.

## 6. RESPONSIBILITY OF THE PARTIES

6.1. For non-performance or improper performance of obligations under this Agreement, the Parties are liable in accordance with the current legislation of the Republic of Belarus.

6.2. The Contractor is not responsible for failures in the operation of third-party services (hosting providers, payment systems, social network APIs) used in the operation of the Customer's website.

6.3. The aggregate liability of the Contractor for any lawsuit or claim is limited to the amount actually paid by the Customer for the corresponding Service.

## 7. PERSONAL DATA

7.1. By accepting the offer, the Customer gives their consent to the collection and processing of their personal data (name, contact phone number, e-mail) by the Contractor for the purpose of fulfilling obligations under the Agreement.
